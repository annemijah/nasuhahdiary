My Study Journey: From School to Degree

Hello! My name is Aina Nasuhah and this is the glimpse of my study journey - starting from school until where I am now, currently on my journey to earn a degree.

My Education Path

  • Primary School - SK Kesang Tua (2012 - 2017): This is where I discovered my love for learning. I joined netball and SPBT duties.
  • Secondary School - SMKA Tun Perak (2018 - 2022): Here, I became more confident. I joined Beaver Computational Thinking.
  • Kolej Matrikulasi Melaka ( 2023 - 2024): Life became more challenging, but I learned time management, independence and how to balance responsibilities.
  • Now - Pursuing My Degree: I am currently pursuing Bachelor's degree in Library Management and I'm excited to continue growing.

Lesson I've Learned

This journey wasn't easy. I faced stress, exam pressure and fear of failure. But with support, consistency and faith, I grew stronger. I learned to be more disciplined and confident.

My Hope

I hope to graduate with a degree and use my knowledge to support and inspire others. Education has shaped who I am, and I'm excited to see where it takes me next.
